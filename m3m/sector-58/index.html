Location Advantage

Sector 58 — The New Ultra-Luxury Epicentre

Golf Course Extension Road's Sector 58 has rapidly emerged as Gurugram's most prestigious residential corridor. Oberoi Realty's debut here with Three Sixty North (₹8,100 Cr sold at launch) confirmed what connoisseurs already knew — this address has arrived.

The sector sits at the intersection of Golf Course Road, Sohna Road, and the Southern Peripheral Road — giving residents effortless connectivity to every key node of the NCR without the congestion of older corridors.

  • Golf Course Extension Road — Gurugram's prestige corridor
  • DLF 5, Cyber Hub, Golf Course Road — 10–15 mins
  • IGI Airport — ~25 km via NH-48 / Dwarka Expressway
  • South Delhi (Vasant Kunj, Saket) — 20–25 mins
  • DPS International, GD Goenka, Pathways Schools nearby
  • Medanta, Fortis, Artemis Hospitals — 10–15 mins
  • Ambience Mall, DLF Mega Mall, South Point Mall — nearby
  • Rapid Metro Sector 55-56 station — connectivity to HUDA City Centre
