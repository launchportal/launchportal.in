About the Builder

M3M India — Magnificent, Magnanimous, Milestone

M3M India is one of the fastest-growing and most celebrated real estate developers in Delhi NCR. Founded in the early 2000s, M3M has redefined luxury living with landmark residential and commercial developments across Gurugram, positioning itself as the premium choice on Golf Course Extension Road and Dwarka Expressway.

From the iconic 56-acre M3M Golf Estate to Trump Towers Gurugram — developed in partnership with the Trump Organization — M3M has consistently delivered award-winning projects that set new benchmarks for design, amenities, and community living.
