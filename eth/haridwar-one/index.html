Project Overview

Haridwar One — Where Spirituality Meets Luxury

Haridwar One is Haridwar's tallest residential project by ETH Infra, located just 2 minutes from Har Ki Pauri. With panoramic vistas of the Ganga River, the city, and the mountains, this residential haven promises an unparalleled living experience blending nature, spirituality, and modern comfort.

The project offers semi-furnished luxury apartments with meticulously planned amenities that cater to the mind, body, and soul. From Meditation Gardens and Yoga Atriums to a Rooftop Club House with breathtaking views, every detail reflects Haridwar's rich spiritual heritage while delivering contemporary luxury.

  • Haridwar's tallest residential tower — iconic skyline presence
  • 2 minutes from Har Ki Pauri — the spiritual heart of Haridwar
  • Panoramic Ganga River & mountain views from upper floors
  • Semi-furnished apartments with premium finishes
  • RERA Registered — UKREP07230000511
  • 24×7 two-tier security with CCTV & biometric lifts
